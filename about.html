About Wisharu

Wisharu is a simple and fun way to create and share Amazon wishlists for any occasion. It helps friends and families stay organized when giving gifts and makes it easy for everyone to see what has already been purchased.

How It Works

  • Create Your Wishlist: Sign in, choose a theme you like, and add your favorite items from Amazon.
  • Share With Others: Send your unique link or code to friends and family. They can open and view your list instantly without needing an account.
  • Live Updates: When someone buys an item from your list, it will show as purchased for others to know what is still available while remaining a secret to you.

Why People Use Wisharu

  • Perfect For Any Occasion: Use it for birthdays, holidays, weddings, fundraisers, or any time you want to organize gifts in one place.
  • Easy To Share: Copy and send your list in a message, email, or post so anyone can see it right away.
  • No Duplicate Gifts: Once an item is marked as purchased, everyone else will know.
  • Personalized Themes: Pick a design that matches your event or style.
  • Direct Amazon Links: Click "Buy Now" to open the product directly on Amazon.

Start creating your own wishlist today and make gift-giving easy for everyone involved.
